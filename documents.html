What's driving the increase in RevPAR?

AI

The 12.3% increase in RevPAR is primarily driven by two factors:

  1. A 5.2% increase in occupancy rate (from 74.6% to 78.5%)
  2. A 6.8% increase in ADR (from $133.29 to $142.35)

The occupancy increase is largely due to stronger corporate bookings (Mon-Thu), while the ADR increase comes from successful implementation of dynamic pricing during peak demand periods and the renovation of premium rooms completed last quarter.
