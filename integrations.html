Connect to Salesforce

Connect INVEST PLATFORM to your Salesforce account to synchronize deals, contacts, and activities.

  1. You will be redirected to Salesforce to authorize this connection.
  2. Log in to your Salesforce account if not already logged in.
  3. Review the permissions requested and click "Allow".
  4. Once authorized, you will be redirected back to complete the setup.

This integration requires permission to:

  • Read your basic profile information
  • Access and manage your opportunities and leads
  • Read your contacts
  • Create and update activities
